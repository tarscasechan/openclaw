# Package Topic

## Goal

Convert research into a strong brief.

## Output

Write a brief with:
- Want: bold hook that opens curiosity
- Need: what must change and why the old shape fails
- Get: the new shape, what it is not, and tradeoffs

## Rules

- Do this before line-level drafting.
- If the structure is weak, restart from concept.
- Keep the brief short and usable.
