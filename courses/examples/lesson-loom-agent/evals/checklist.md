# Lesson Loom Evaluation Checklist

Passes if:
- Course has 5-8 lessons.
- Each lesson teaches one concept.
- Each lesson title is verb-led and keyword-included.
- Each lesson ends with a `But:` hook.
- The course builds one visible artifact.
- The artifact is useful on its own, not merely a toy mechanics demo.
- The learner does not have to invent the application after finishing.
- A failure/edge case appears before the end.
- There is a smoke test and cut list.

Fails if:
- It becomes an encyclopedia.
- It introduces concepts before the pain that earns them.
- It keeps advanced material needed only after first success.
- The artifact cannot be tested.
- The artifact is a hammer with no nail.
