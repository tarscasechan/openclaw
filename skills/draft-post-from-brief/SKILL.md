# Draft Post From Brief

## Goal

Turn a brief into a readable draft.

## Rules

- Lead with the point.
- Use progressive disclosure.
- Keep paragraphs short.
- Stay close to the brief.
- Do not over-polish.

## Output

A draft that can move to demo and edit passes.
